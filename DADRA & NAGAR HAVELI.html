DADRA & NAGAR HAVELI-D LAND OF NATURAL BEAUTY

Vapi

Popularly known as the ‘City of Chemicals.’ This city is located near the banks of the River Damanganga. However, the most popular place in the city is called square, where the majority of the people hang out and spend a good time together. Places like Jampore beach and Devka Beach are popular among the locals.

Dadra

Located a few kilometres from north of Gujarat, Dadra is a small town with beautiful tourist attractions. Places like Dadra Garden and BAPS Shri Narayan Temple are among the few attractions.

Khanvel

If you are someone who loves nature, Khanvel is one place you shouldn’t miss out on. The beautiful scenic views from the hills is a view you should witness. One can also witness the river Sakartod flow through the hills. This place is indeed a travellers paradise. Khanvel is an ideal place to enjoy the serene atmosphere and witness elegance, like no other place.

Satmaliya deer Park

As the name implies, this place is home to various deer species such as spotted deer, Black Buck, Nilgai and many more. The lush green park is beguiling to look at. This place would show you how beautiful Mother Nature is and get you up close. Needless to say, this place attracts plenty of tourists regularly. One can explore this place with the help of a safari.

Hirva Van Garden

This place is known for its lush greenery and cascading waterfalls. It is said that the garden is devoted to the tribal God known as Hirva. One can spend quality time with family and friends, by enjoying the greenery and the view of the waterfalls.

Bindrabin

Bindrabin is said to be one of the holiest places in Dadra and Nagar Haveli. This place is home to the famous Bindrabin temple. It is situated near the banks of the river Sakartod. The Deity of this temple is Lord Shiva. The temple is visited by plenty of tourists regularly.
