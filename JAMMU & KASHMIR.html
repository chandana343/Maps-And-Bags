JAMMU & KASHMIR-CHALO KASHMIR

Yusmarg

Yusmarg is a hill station in the western part of the valley of Kashmir. The Ahmadiyya Muslim community believes it to be the place where Jesus once lived. The landscape here is more beautiful than anything you will ever see.

Gulmarg

Gulmarg has everything going for it: snowdressed mountains, greenery, lakes, pine & fir forests and different types of flowers.

Pahalgam

Any tourist visiting the top 10 places in Jammu and Kashmir will always visit Pahalgam. Located in the Anantnag district, it is located on the banks of the River Lidder.

Srinagar

Srinagar finds a place on this list because it is one of the most famous tourist destinations in Jammu and Kashmir as well as the top honeymoon destination.

Doda

Doda is a district located in the eastern part of Jammu and has plenty of opportunities for sightseeing and even adventure.

Sanasar

Sanasar is named after the villages of Sana and Sar, and can be counted among the most beautiful places to visit in Jammu and Kashmir. It is a meadow situated in Jammu, and has the appearance of a cup.
